Research Statement

My research interests lie in Computer Vision, Generative AI, and Human-AI. My research focuses on understanding and generating images, videos, and 3D/4D content, with an emphasis on narrative-driven multimedia such as movies. Ultimately, I aim to build AI systems that can understand and interact with the physical world.
